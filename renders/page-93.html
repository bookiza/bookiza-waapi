Uranus has a total of 27 moons and a very light planetary ring system.

Top five notable moons are named Titania, Miranda, Ariel, Umbriel and Oberon. Titania is the largest among them with a radius of only ~490 miles. Miranda on the other hand has a very unique surface. Miranda sports a 12.5 mile high scarp Verona Rupes that is probably the tallest cliff in the Solar System.
